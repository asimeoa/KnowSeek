# 🚀 KnowSeek Business Strategy

## 1. Vision
"Stop searching. Find your own knowledge and use it."
KnowSeek aims to be the leading on-premise AI knowledge platform for German SMEs (Mittelstand) in the automotive and manufacturing sectors.

## 2. Target Audience (The "Sweet Spot")
* **SMEs (KMU):** 50–500 employees.
* **Industries:** Automotive, Engineering, Logistics.
* **Problem:** Expert knowledge is buried in old PDFs, CAD files, and technical drawings.

## 3. Core Value Proposition (The "Why")
* **On-Premise Security:** Unlike ChatGPT, KnowSeek runs on the customer's own hardware. No data leak, no cloud risk.
* **Industry Specific:** Deep integration for geometry search (PartSeek) and technical standards (StandardSeek).
* **Cost Efficiency:** Reduces R&D costs by up to 18% through reuse of existing components.

## 4. Why Now? (The 2026 Edge)
* **Digital Transformation:** German SMEs are forced to automate to stay competitive.
* **AI Maturation:** High-performance AI models can now run locally on standard server hardware.
* **IP Protection:** Growing concern about intellectual property in the cloud makes on-premise solutions the only choice for many OEMs.
