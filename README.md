# KnowSeek 🧠
> **Stop searching. Find your own knowledge and use it!**

KnowSeek is a modular intelligence platform designed for the automotive and manufacturing industry (SMEs). It connects your company's hidden data into one central, searchable brain – **100% on-premise and secure.**

---

## 🛠 The 4 Core Modules

* **🔍 PartSeek:** Find existing parts by geometry and metadata. Stop reinventing the wheel.
* **📄 DocumentSeek:** Instant access to reports, manuals, and customer presentations.
* **📏 StandardSeek:** Automatic compliance check for technical norms and OEM standards.
* **💰 CostSeek:** Real-time price feedback during the design phase (Design-to-Cost).

---

## 🔒 Security & Privacy
* **On-Premise:** The AI runs locally on your server. No data leaves the building.
* **GDPR Compliant:** Built to meet the highest European data standards.
* **IP Protection:** Your intellectual property is safe and stays your advantage.

## 📈 Why KnowSeek?
* **-18% Development Costs** through part reuse.
* **Faster Onboarding** for new engineers.
* **Zero Risk:** No cloud, no external data processing.
